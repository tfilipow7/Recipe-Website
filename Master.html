Vegan Pancakes

This batter will make soft pancakes with a thin, cripsy crust.

Prep Time Total Time Servings
15 Minutes 45 Minutes 3-4 People

Quantity Ingredient Quantity Ingredient
1 1/4 Cup Flour 1/2 Teas Salt
2 Tables Sugar 2 Teas Baking Powder
1 1/4 Cup Water 1 Tables Oil

Step 1: Sift and & Combine Dry Ingredients in a mixing bowl. Start preheating skillet on low heat with a bit of oil on it.
Step 2: Add wet ingredients and use a whisk to gently mix ingredients together. DO NOT OVERMIX. Batter will small have lumps, these will cook out.
Step 3:Use a 1/4 cup to pour out pancakes on the preheated skillet. Adjust tempurature. For best results do not compress pancakes between flips, this makes dense instead of fluffy.
